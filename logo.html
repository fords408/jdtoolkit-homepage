Logo! <3

Here is the logo I made for class. I will probably make another one some day, but this was just a trial for practice.

What I did for the assignment:

On Adobe Illustrator, I created a logo for a flower company of my dreams called "Fordi Flowers". Using the circle tool, I created a few circles and layered them on top of one another in a flower pattern, and used a few different shades of blue and teal to make a beauuuuutiful flower that would be the face of my fake flower shop.

I also used a few fonts to make this logo — including one that only uses flowers as characters.
